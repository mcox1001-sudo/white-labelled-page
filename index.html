Core Capabilities

Everything a CPMS Should Do.
And More.

Arcane gives you a full suite charge point management system with native field service, energy management and driver app, all under one roof. No bolt ons, no third parties, no surprises.

⚡

OCPP Charge Point Control

Full OCPP 1.6J and 2.0.1 support. Remote start, stop, reset, firmware updates and diagnostics for every charger on your network, regardless of manufacturer.

📊

Real Time Monitoring

Live dashboards showing charger status, energy consumption, session data and fault alerts. Know what is happening across your entire estate at a glance.

💰

Tariff and Billing Management

Flexible tariff structures, PAYG, subscription and fleet billing. Integrated payment processing with full revenue reporting and automated invoicing.

🔒

User and Access Control

RFID, app based and contactless authentication. Manage driver groups, corporate fleets and public access policies from a single interface.

🔧

Field Service Management

Built in job scheduling, engineer dispatch and asset tracking. Preventative maintenance, SLA management and mobile workforce tools, all native, no third party bolt ons.

🌱

Energy and Load Management

Dynamic load balancing, solar integration and smart scheduling. Optimise energy costs across your sites and prepare for flexibility market participation.

📱

Branded Driver App

Your own branded mobile app for drivers. Charger discovery, session management, payment and support, all carrying your logo and colours.

🚀

Roaming and Interoperability

OCPI roaming support connects your network to major roaming platforms. Let drivers from other networks charge on your points and vice versa.

📈

Reporting and Analytics

Detailed usage analytics, revenue reports, uptime tracking and carbon savings. Export ready data for stakeholder reporting and grant compliance.
